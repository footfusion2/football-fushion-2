## 🔧 Common Issues and Fixes

### ❌ "QB aimbot throws to the wrong receiver"
**Fix:** The **football fusion 2 script qb** aimbot selects targets based on a separation score that factors distance, coverage proximity, and route depth. If it's consistently targeting a suboptimal receiver, open *Settings* → *QB Aimbot* → *Target Priority* and switch from *Auto* to *Nearest Open* or *Deepest Route* based on your preferred play style. Also verify you're on the latest script version — receiver scoring models are updated after every FF2 physics patch.

---

### ❌ "Ball magnet isn't pulling passes"
**Fix:** Verify the **football fusion 2 script mag** is enabled and your character is in the receiver role — the magnet is position-conditional and only activates when your character is in an eligible catch state. If mag is enabled and you're in the correct role but passes still aren't being pulled, check your configured range — a range below 15 studs may be too small for typical pass trajectories. Increase to 25–35 studs and test.

---

### ❌ "Auto catch triggers but the animation plays without completing"
**Fix:** This occurs when the catch interaction fires correctly but the server-side validation rejects it due to the ball being out of the legitimate catch radius. Increase your **football fusion 2 script mag** range so the ball is closer to your character before auto catch fires — the two systems work best together with mag pulling the ball into close range before auto catch completes the interaction.

---

### ❌ "Script GUI doesn't appear on iOS after execution"
**Fix:** The **football fusion 2 script ios** GUI initializes after a brief delay on some iOS devices. Wait 5–8 seconds after execution before assuming it failed. If the panel still doesn't appear, tap the top-left corner of the screen — the GUI may have initialized off-screen. Go to *Settings* → *GUI Position* → *Reset* to force it back to the default center position.

---

### ❌ "Speed boost is causing desync on the server"
**Fix:** High speed multipliers (above 2.5x) can cause server-side position desync in *Football Fusion 2*, making your character appear to teleport to other players and triggering rubber-banding on your client. Reduce your speed multiplier to 1.3x–1.8x for stable server-synchronized movement. If desync persists at lower values, enable *Smooth Acceleration* in *Settings* → *Speed* to ramp speed gradually rather than instantly.

---

### ❌ "Auto run routes sends my character in the wrong direction"
**Fix:** Auto run routes calculates route paths based on your starting position and the detected play formation. If the formation detection misreads your position (common in custom or non-standard plays), the route path will be incorrect. Toggle *Auto Run Routes* off and back on before the snap to force a fresh formation read. For non-standard play types, use manual positioning and only enable auto routes for standard offensive formations.

---

### ❌ "No-tackle stops working mid-game"
**Fix:** The no-tackle system can lose its state reference after a server-side tackle animation is committed before the protection layer responds — this happens on high-latency servers. Toggle the feature off and back on to reinstate protection. Enable *Pre-emptive Mode* in *Settings* → *No Tackle* — this activates protection earlier in the tackle detection cycle, reducing the latency window that allows committed tackles to bypass it.
